# Food Save

This app aims to reduce food waste by connecting restaurants and consumers so that surplus food that is still edible can be sold at a discount, rather than being thrown away.

## 🎯 Goals
- Reduce food waste
- Help restaurants minimize losses
- Provide more affordable food for the community
- Support SDGs 2 (Zero Hunger) & SDGs 12 (Responsible Consumption & Production)

## 👥 Target Users
- Restaurants / food MSMEs
- Students & the general public
- Social communities

## 🚀 Core Features
- Upload & list surplus food
- Purchase & payment
- Scheduled pickup system
- Impact tracking (food saved)
- Business dashboard

## 🌱 Impact
With this app, food is not wasted and can provide economic, social, and environmental benefits.
